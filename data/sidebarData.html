Bear rescue centre "Nadiya"
FOUR PAWS set up this centre in Zhytomyr in 2012. Five bears currently live there: the two young bears Nastia and Bodia, former baiting bear Masha, and Zoya and Julia, ex-circus bears that had later been kept in private hands

What help is needed
"Since our creation in the 1980s, we have worked tirelessly to help animals across the globe. Our aim is simple. We want to end the mistreatment of animals under human control, both now and in the future. But we can't do any of this without your kind support. Thank you."
